About Us

The BAWDF company is located at the Brno-Turany airport and is the sole proprietor of cargo handling services. With a wide variety of equipment ranging from small forklifts to highloaders or even cranes there is nothing we are unable to load. We also provide cattering to charter and private flights with almost unlimited food choices.
